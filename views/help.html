How to play

Mastermind is a game where you have to guess the color of the squares. You have x attempts to guess the correct combination of colors.

Rules

In this game you have to attempt to guess a convination of the colors selected for the play

Colors

There are 6 colors to choose from: red, blue, green, yellow, purple and orange.

Board

The board is composed of 4 squares, where you can change the color of each square by clicking on them. It will show all your attempts in the game

Clues

After each attempt, the game will give you clues to help you guess the correct combination. The game have 3 icons to give you the clues needed to win

Circle

Means that you have selected a color that isn't in the convination

Interrogant

Means that you have selected a color that is in the convination, but it's in the wrong position

Check

Means that you have selected a color that is in the convination and it's in the right position

Difficulties

  • Easy: 4 squares, 4 colors, 10 attempts
  • Medium: 4 squares, 6 colors, 8 attempts
  • Hard: 4 squares, 6 colors, 6 attempts
